## ‚úçÔ∏è Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice Characteristics
1. **Professional yet Approachable** - Knowledgeable without being stuffy
2. **Empowering** - "You can do this" energy
3. **Clear & Direct** - No jargon, no fluff
4. **Confident** - We know what we're doing
5. **Privacy-Conscious** - Always respectful of user data

### Writing Guidelines

#### Do's ‚úÖ
- Use active voice: "Track your applications" not "Applications can be tracked"
- Be specific: "Land your dream job in 6 weeks" not "Find success faster"
- Address user directly: "You" and "Your"
- Keep sentences short and scannable
- Use bullet points for lists
- Include emojis sparingly for visual interest (‚ú® üéØ üîí ‚òÅÔ∏è ü§ñ)

#### Don'ts ‚ùå
- Avoid corporate jargon: "synergy", "leverage", "paradigm"
- Don't oversell: "Revolutionary", "Game-changing"
- Never compromise on privacy messaging
- Don't use confusing technical terms without explanation
- Avoid passive voice
- Don't make promises you can't keep

### Example Messaging

#### Homepage Hero
**Good:** "Find Your Next Role with Confidence"
**Bad:** "Revolutionize Your Career Trajectory"

#### Feature Description
**Good:** "Track applications, manage resumes, and organize your job search with military-grade encryption."
**Bad:** "Leverage our synergistic platform to optimize your career journey."

#### Call to Action
**Good:** "Get Started Free"
**Bad:** "Commence Your Journey"
